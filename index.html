About BCS Builders

Based in the heart of Morabadi, Ranchi, BCS Builders has been a cornerstone of local construction and development for over a decade. We are a team of passionate professionals dedicated to creating spaces that are not just buildings, but homes and thriving business environments. Our philosophy is simple: combine modern architectural design with robust engineering and transparent processes.

Following in the entrepreneurial footsteps of their father, who established a renowned clothing business in the heart of Ranchi, brothers Aman and Abhi Gupta bring a legacy of quality and trust to the construction industry.
